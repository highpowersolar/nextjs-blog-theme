When it comes to solar panels, there are a lot of pros and cons to consider. Solar energy is a great alternative to traditional energy sources, but it’s also a big investment. So, is it worth it to install solar panels at your home?

Here are some of the pros and cons of installing a residential solar panel system:

PROS
- Solar energy is a renewable resource, so you're helping to reduce your reliance on fossil fuels.
- Solar panels can significantly reduce your electricity bills.
- Solar panel systems add value to your home.
- Solar energy is relatively low-maintenance once it's installed.

CONS
- Solar panel systems can be expensive to install.
- Solar panels require a sunny location to work optimally.
- Solar panel systems are low-maintenance cost but it require periodic maintenance.
